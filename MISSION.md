# MISSION

**Core Goal:**
Develop a Company Finance & Revenue Sharing CRM (Shohoj Ledger) to manage income, expenses, reserve balances, member loans, due balances, and monthly settlements. The system ensures that only paid/partial income is included in settlement, and shares are calculated properly between the CEO, Developers, and Company (reserve balance).

**Current Status:**

- **Last major feature added:** Completed Phase 2 (Core Financial Operations), implementing APIs and UIs for Income (with partial payment tracking), Expenses (with approval workflows), and Projects (with Profitability & Loss calculation).
- **Next Phase:** Phase 3 (Funds, Reserves & Advances) focusing on non-shareable funds, reserve transaction records, and employee loans.
